Privacy Policy for Fairway Caddie AI

Effective date: June 9, 2026

Fairway Caddie AI is an iPhone-first prototype for a wearable golf caddie concept. The current prototype uses fictional local demo data and simulated wearable status.

Information collected

The current prototype does not collect personal information and does not send user data to an owner-operated server.

Local prototype data

The app may keep temporary in-app state during a demo session, such as selected demo hole, simulated shot position, caddie messages, and shot notes. This data is used only to run the local prototype flow and is not uploaded to an owner-operated backend.

Location, Bluetooth, microphone, and speech

The current prototype does not request Location Services, Bluetooth, Microphone, or Speech Recognition permissions.

Golf position, distance, wearable status, course information, and caddie responses are simulated with local fictional demo data.

Accounts and backend services

The app does not include account creation, login, cloud sync, analytics, advertising, subscriptions, or payment features in this prototype build.

Real course data

The current prototype does not use real golf course data. Demo course names, holes, hazards, yardages, and guidance are fictional and intended for concept review.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the app's behavior changes. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when changes are made.

Support

Support for this prototype is handled through TestFlight feedback, App Store reviews, and official app review channels.
