Fairway Caddie AI

Fairway Caddie AI is an iPhone-first prototype for a wearable golf caddie concept.

Prototype behavior

  • Uses fictional local course data for a short guided demo.
  • Simulates a hat-clip-style wearable signal without real hardware.
  • Shows terrain-aware yardage, hazard context, deterministic caddie responses, shot notes, and a summary.
  • Does not use real GPS, Bluetooth, location tracking, microphone access, accounts, or backend services in this prototype build.
