Privacy Policy for Glass Skin Routine

Effective date: May 21, 2026

Glass Skin Routine is an Android app for tracking simple cosmetic skincare routines.

Information the app stores locally

The app lets you save routine steps, checklist progress, product names, opened dates, and product notes.

This information is stored locally on your device. The app does not require an account and does not send your routine or product information to a developer-operated server.

Permissions

The app does not request access to your camera, photos, contacts, precise location, microphone, SMS, Health Connect, or health records.

The app uses internet access for ads and consent or privacy messaging.

Advertising and consent

The app uses Google AdMob to show ads and Google User Messaging Platform to manage consent where required.

Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect and share information such as approximate location inferred from IP address, app interactions, diagnostics, and device or other identifiers, including the Android advertising ID and app set ID. Google states that this data is used for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, security, and compliance, and that data is encrypted in transit.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its advertising services at:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

You can manage or reset your Android advertising ID in Android system settings. Where available, the app also shows privacy options provided by Google's consent SDK.

Accounts

The app does not allow users to create accounts and does not support logging in with an external account.

Health and beauty disclaimer

Glass Skin Routine provides general cosmetic routine tracking and beauty tips. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or guaranteed skincare results.

For serious, persistent, or medical skin concerns, consult a qualified professional.

Data deletion

Because the app does not create user accounts or store data on a developer-operated server, there is no account deletion process. You can remove locally stored app data by using the in-app reset option, clearing the app's storage, or uninstalling the app from your device.

For data handled by Google advertising services, use the privacy controls provided by Google and Android.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the app's behavior or third-party services change. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when changes are made.
