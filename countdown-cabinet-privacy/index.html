Privacy Policy for Countdown Cabinet

Effective date: May 31, 2026

Countdown Cabinet is an Android-first countdown and event reminder app for keeping important dates visible.

Information the app stores locally

The app lets you save event titles, target dates, optional target times, categories, yearly repeat choices, optional notes, reminder choices, and Android widget visibility choices.

This information is stored locally on your device. The app does not require an account and does not send your countdown or event information to a developer-operated server.

Notifications

If you choose a reminder for an event, the app may ask for notification permission and schedule a local notification on your device.

Notifications are optional. You can use the app without enabling notifications.

Android home-screen widget

If you mark an event for the Android widget, the event title, category, date, and countdown label may appear on your home screen. Optional notes are not included in widget snapshots.

You control which events are visible in the widget.

Permissions

The current app build may request notification permission only when you enable reminders. It does not request access to your camera, photos, contacts, calendar, precise location, microphone, SMS, Health Connect, health records, or files.

Advertising and consent

The current pre-launch build does not include ads, analytics, account login, cloud sync, or a developer-operated backend.

If ads are added in a later public build, this policy and the store data disclosures will be updated before release. Google AdMob or related consent SDKs may collect SDK data when integrated, as described by Google.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its advertising services at:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Accounts

The app does not allow users to create accounts and does not support logging in with an external account.

Data deletion

Because the app does not create user accounts or store data on a developer-operated server, there is no account deletion process. You can remove locally stored app data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app from your device.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the app's behavior or third-party services change. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when changes are made.
